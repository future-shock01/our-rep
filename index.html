NIGERIA MOROCCAN CHAMBER OF COMMERCE (NMCCM)

The NMCCM is citizen centric business organization representing companies dedicated to mentorship across every sector of the economy, doing business in USA, Morocco, Nigeria, and other supporting Nations. Our members range from the National Governments , corporations, mentorship communities, and affiliate chambers of commerce who converge to contribute and benefit from diplomatic business advantages and enterprise mentorship
