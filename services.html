ECOWAS Trade Liberalisation Scheme

The ECOWAS Trade Liberalisation Scheme (ETLS) is the main ECOWAS operational tool for promoting the West Africa region as a Free Trade Area. This Lies in tandem with the one of the objective of the community which is the establishment of a common market through "the Liberalisation of trade by abolition, among Memebers States, of customs duties levied on imports and exports, and the abolition among Member States, of non-tariff barriers"

NACCIMA assits in the ETLS Application process for Enquires: send and email to info@naccima.com

NAFDAC

The National Agency For Food and Drug Administration and Control (NAFDAC) is to regulate and control the manufacture, importation, exportation, distribution, advertisment, sale and use of food, Drugs, cosmetics, medical devices, packaged water, chemicals and detergents. NACCIMA is assists in processing product Registration and Regulation.
